About Me

Making Data
make sense

I have long been fascinated with making sense of data. My interest first began during my PhD research, where I analyzed data on human perception. During this project, I discovered that each dataset has a story to tell.

This is precisely why data fascinates me, each and every dataset is unique, and has the potential to produce groundbreaking insights. Over time, I developed a passion for the process of turning columns and rows into value.

Being able to dig up patterns, trends, and relationships, hidden within data is simply what I enjoy doing. This is what inspired me to become a Data Analyst. Ever since I made this decision, I’ve been hooked, and haven’t looked back.
